int iDynTree::Regressors::DynamicsRegressorParameter::elemIndex

Depending on the category, the parameter will referent to an element (such as a link or a sensor) throught an integer.

This integer should be interpreted with respect to the serialization provided by the SensorList class or the UndirectedTree class.
